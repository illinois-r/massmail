Apply To Be Part of The Illinois Student Government!
March 10, 2026 10:32 PM

Hello Illini!

From all of us here at Illinois Student Council, we would like to wish you luck on your midterms and a good spring break! This part of the semester can be hard for many, so please make sure you’re taking care of yourself! 

We are currently in the process of taking applications for the next group of Student Leaders in our campus. If you want to get involved in the newly renamed Illinois Student Government, we encourage you to apply within the coming weeks!

Apply to Be Part of The Executive Branch!

As Anjali and I’s time in this role comes to an end, the new administration will need a team to start building their vision for Illinois! Members of the Executive are chosen by the President and Vice President, and are responsible for the planning and implementation of ISG events and initiatives. 

If you are interested in being a Director or Coordinator on the Executive Branch, please apply here: https://cglink.me/2sM/s651

Apply to Be Part of The Legislative Branch!

The Legislative Branch is the primary deliberative body of the Illinois Student Government. To facilitate its work and to help guide its decision making, the Legislative Branch is accepting applications for a Secretary, Parliamentarian, and Archivist, as well as committee Members At-Large. 

If you are interested in serving in the Legislative Branch please apply here: https://cglink.me/2sM/s654

Apply to Be Part of The Judicial Branch!

The Judicial Branch is the system that keeps ISG accountable. It is composed of one Chief Justice as well as other Justices that hear cases of misconduct and provide recommendations of action to the Illinois Student Government.

If you are interested in joining The Judicial Branch please apply here: https://cglink.me/2sM/s655

Apply to an External Appointment!

Illinois Student Government is responsible for recommending qualified students to the following campus boards and committees: 

  • Campus Administrative Manual Advisory Committee
  • Chancellor's Special Events Advisory Committee 
  • Commencement Committee
  • Student Election Board
  • Illini Union Board
  • Illinois App Steering Committee
  • Student Fee Advisory Committee

If you are interested in being the ISG appointment to any of these boards or committees, please apply here: https://cglink.me/2sM/s656

We hope you apply so we can keep making meaningful changes on campus! 

I-L-L,

Gabi DalSanto, Anjali Kumar, and the Illinois Student Council
