Annual Security and Fire Safety Report
October 1, 2024 3:51 PM

Dear campus community member:

It is my pleasure to share the 2024 Annual Security and Fire Safety Report with you. Each year, we publish this report which contains important crime statistics and information from the previous three years concerning campus property, certain off-campus buildings or on property owned or controlled by the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Additionally, the report includes statistics about fires in campus student housing, fire safety policies and procedures, campus safety policies, and other safety information. 

The University of Illinois Division of Public Safety and Campus Code Compliance and Fire Safety prioritize providing students, faculty and staff with a safe and secure environment to work, study and conduct business. However, we cannot do it alone. Crime and fire prevention is a community effort, and by reading this report, you are taking proactive measures to protect the safety of yourself and others. 

In compliance with the Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Crime Statistics Act and federal regulations, the full report is available online at police.illinois.edu/clery/report/. 

A printed copy of the report is also available via request at the U. of I. Public Safety Building, located at 1110 W. Springfield Ave., Urbana, IL 61801, or by contacting us at 217-333-1216. You are also welcome to review the Daily Crime Log. 

To further enhance your personal safety, we encourage you to:

● Sign up for Illini-Alert text messages
● Familiarize yourself with campus safety measures
● Take a moment to prepare for emergencies
● Review sex-based misconduct and violence resources
● Learn fire safety tips and procedures

By utilizing these resources, we can work together to maintain a safer campus, allowing our educational, research, and public service missions to thrive.

Thank you for your commitment to campus safety.

Sincerely,
Matt Ballinger
Executive Director of Public Safety/Chief of Police

Mike Brown
Campus Code Compliance and Fire Safety
