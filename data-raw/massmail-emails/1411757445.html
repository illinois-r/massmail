Final days to donate to CCFD this year
November 14, 2022 10:02 AM

Dear Colleagues,

As we enter this final week of the 2022 Campus Charitable Fund Drive, I want to express my sincere gratitude to every one of you who has made a gift that will support others in our community. I cannot think of a better way to usher in the coming holiday seasons than with your demonstration of compassion and care for one another.

For those of you who want to join thousands of your colleagues and coworkers in making a gift this year, I would remind you there are still three days left to do so. This year’s campaign will close at the end of the workday on Wednesday, Nov. 16, so you have some time to donate. I do hope you will consider taking a few minutes to follow this link and see all of the opportunities you have to make a difference with your gift. Whether you choose to donate by check, a direct gift or automatic payroll deduction, you’ll be supporting great causes in your own community.

I realize this is a time of year when many fundraising campaigns are in full swing and you have many worthy choices for your charitable dollars. But I am extremely proud to say we have raised more than $1 million so far and that is an enormous accomplishment. Thank you to the thousands who have already given and my deepest gratitude to the unit and section leaders who have worked tirelessly to make this campaign a success.

Thank you all so much for your investments in others this year through the Campus Charitable Fund Drive.

Sincerely,

Robert J. Jones
Chancellor
