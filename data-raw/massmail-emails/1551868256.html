2022-2023 Student Insurance Program Changes
September 2, 2022 3:31 PM

Dear Graduate and Undergraduate Students,

Your healthcare benefits are important, which is why we want to share with you the following changes to the Student Insurance Program for the 2022-2023 academic year. With these changes, we are pleased to be able to offer voluntary dental and vision coverage more broadly than in past years. Please review the information below, including the information on the website links provided.

Medical Plan

UnitedHealthcare StudentResources will continue to insure medical benefits for students and their dependents, with no change to benefits or to semester fees. Fall enrollment dates are August 19 through September 30, 2022.

Dental Plan

The dental plan carrier will change in the 2022-2023 academic year from Delta Dental to UnitedHealthcare StudentResources.

The Urbana campus will now offer two student dental plans.

  1. One dental plan automatically covers Graduate Assistants and Fellows with waiver-generating appointments beginning in the fall term. Such students may elect to voluntarily enroll dependents by going to the Student Insurance website starting today through October 4, 2022 and coverage will be effective on August 20. Students with waiver-generating appointments after October 4 can be enrolled in the dental plan as of the date their appointment is processed in accordance with the plan’s qualifying life event provisions.

  2. A separate, voluntary plan for undergraduate and graduate students is being introduced for such students to directly purchase (and enroll dependents) with UnitedHealthcare at: uhcsr.com from August 20 through October 4, 2022, with coverage effective on August 20, 2022.

Vision Plan

The EyeMed Fidelity Security Life vision plan will continue to be provided to Graduate Assistants and Fellows with waiver-generating appointments. Coverage for dependents may be purchased by going to the Student Insurance website.  

New this academic year for undergraduates and graduate students is the option to voluntarily purchase vision benefits for themselves and their dependents. Coverage is purchased on the Student Insurance website.

Enrollment for the vision plan is available now until October 4, 2022, with an effective date of coverage of September 1, 2022.

Fees for the medical, dental, and vision plans for undergraduate and graduate students without waiver-generating appointments are located on the Student Insurance website at: si.illinois.edu/fees.  Graduate Assistants and Fellows with waiver-generating appointments may find the dental fees at grad.illinois.edu/current/dental and the vision fees at grad.illinois.edu/current/vision. 

Direct any questions to Student Insurance at 217-333-0165 and visit the Student Insurance website at: si.illinois.edu.
