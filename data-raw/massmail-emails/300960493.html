Campus Student Elections - Application Deadline Extended
February 10, 2023 4:02 PM

MASSMAIL - Campus Student Elections - Spring 2023 Elections (Application Deadline Extended)

 The Campus Student Election Commission (CSEC) has extended the Application Deadline to run in the Spring 2023 Campus Student Elections or to submit a referendum question.  Applications are now due Wednesday, February 15, at 11:59 p.m

Candidate applications and referendum submissions for the Spring 2023 election are available on our website, https://studentelections.illinois.edu/  These must be submitted no later than Wednesday, February 15, 2023, at 11:59 p.m. Voting will take place online from Thursday, March 23 through Friday, March 24, 2023. Students can apply to run for any of the following positions:

  • Illinois Student Government (ISG) Executive:
    1. Student Body President &  Student Body Vice-President
  •  Student Senators:
    1.  Illinois Student Government (ISG)
    2.  U-C Senate
  • Student Organization Resource Fee (SORF) Board
  • Student Trustee

To apply for any of these positions, visit https://studentelections.illinois.edu/elections/ to fill out an application.

If you have any questions, please contact the CSEC at studentelections@illinois.edu. If you are not interested in running for an elected position, you may apply to join the CSEC at our website.
