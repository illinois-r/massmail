Last Month to Create Illinois Wiki Sites
May 30, 2023 10:02 AM

As previously announced, the Illinois Wiki service will be retired by June 30, 2024. The retirement is happening in three phases and the first deadline is approaching next month.

The following dates provide a timeline of retirement plans:

  • June 30, 2023 – No new sites can be created
  • January 1, 2024 – Illinois Wiki set to read-only
  • June 30, 2024 – Full Illinois Wiki retirement

Visit the Illinois Wiki Retirement page to view FAQs and identify an alternative to the wiki. If you need more help, please request a Wiki consult or contact the Tech Services Help Desk at consult@illinois.edu or by creating a Tech Request.
