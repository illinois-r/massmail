2025 Campus Student Elections Begins Today at Noon
February 25, 2025 9:31 AM

Voting for the Spring 2025 Student Elections will open today at Noon. The ballot will become available at 12:00 PM on Tuesday, February 25, and will close at 12:00 PM on Thursday, February 27, for all student elected positions. We encourage you to vote at One Illinois!

Spring 2025 elections consist of the following:

  • The Student Trustee from the Urbana-Champaign Campus to the Board of Trustees of the University of Illinois System
  • Illinois Student Council (ISC) Student Body President and Vice President
  • Illinois Student Council (ISC) Student Representative – 44 seats representing all colleges
  • University Senator – 50 seats representing all colleges
  • 14 students to membership on the Student Organization Resource Fee Board (SORF)
  • All referenda submitted to a vote of the students of the Urbana-Champaign campus at the University of Illinois

Instructions to Access the Ballot:

  1. To access your Student Election Ballot, login to OneIllinois (one.illinois.edu).

  2. If it is the first time you have accessed OneIllinois, please follow the prompts to set up your profile.

  3. Once you have completed the profile setup, you may access the ballot by clicking on the Student Elections graphic below the OneIllinois banner.

  4. If you need assistance accessing your OneIllinois account, please email the OneIllinois Team at one@illinois.edu.

If you have any questions about the election process, please contact the CSEC at studentelections@illinois.edu.
