Road Closures for Illinois Race Weekend
April 21, 2026 2:02 PM

Dear Students, Staff, and Faculty 

As we approach Illinois Race Weekend, please be aware of road closures affecting our community from Thursday evening through Saturday. All races, including the full marathon, will start and end on our campus, which will result in temporary traffic disruptions. 

This is a weekend that really shows off our local community as thousands of runners, volunteers, and spectators gather across Champaign-Urbana. To ensure you can plan accordingly, race organizers have compiled comprehensive information: 

  • Road Closures: Beginning Thursday, April 23, several roads in Urbana, Champaign, and Savoy will be temporarily closed to accommodate the races. For detailed maps of affected streets, the weekend event schedule, navigation tips, parking restrictions, and more, please refer to the Illinois Marathon website. 
  • Changes to Bus Routes: Due to these road closures, some bus routes may experience temporary alterations. Stay updated by checking with the Champaign-Urbana Mass Transit District for any changes to your regular routes. 

Thank you in advance for your understanding and cooperation. If you’re planning to cheer on participants, please do so safely and enjoy the events. 

Sincerely, 

Aimee Heeter 
Senior Vice Chancellor for Finance and Administration
