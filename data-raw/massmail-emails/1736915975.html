Call for Civil Service Professional Nominations to the Search Committee to Advise the Chancellor on the Selection of a Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs & Provost

The Senate Committee on Committees is seeking civil service professional nominations to the Search Committee to Advise the Chancellor on the Selection of a Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs & Provost.

Please submit nominations no later than 11:59 pm on Friday, July 22, 2022.

Nominations should be submitted via the online form: https://forms.illinois.edu/sec/990430145

Civil Service Professional Member (1):

The Senate shall elect one (1) civil service professional member. Committee on Committees shall nominate two civil service professionals.

Thank you for your assistance. We look forward to receiving nominations.
