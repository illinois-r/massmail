Join Illinois Student Council | 2025-2026 Leadership Opportunities
March 23, 2025 7:02 PM

Illinois Student Council (ISC) is the university’s official form of student government, advocating for the betterment of our campus community with integrity and transparency. For this 2025-2026 academic year, we aim to advocate for students and see real, sustainable change happening on our campus. If this interests you, we have tons of opportunities ahead! All applications open on Monday, March 24th. 

Leadership Opportunities

Administrative Officers
Administrative Officers provide leadership and oversight of operational aspects while advocating for students with the university administration, Champaign-Urbana community, and local, state, and federal governments. Learn more about Administrative Officers.

  • Treasurer
  • Chief of Staff
  • Director of Administration
  • Director of Communication and Marketing
  • Director of Community & Governmental Relations
  • Director of Engagement

Access the Administrative Officer application! Application closes Friday, April 4th at Noon.

Coordinators & Liaisons

Coordinators and Liaisons are specialized positions that provide opportunities for students to contribute to the mission of ISC in specific ways. Learn more about Coordinators and Liaisons.

  • Academics Coordinator
  • Alumni Relations Coordinator
  • Campus Safety Coordinator
  • Champaign City Liaison
  • Champaign County Liaison
  • Civic Engagement Coordinator
  • Communications Coordinator
  • Design Coordinator 
  • Diversity, Equity & Inclusion Coordinator
  • Environmental Sustainability Coordinator
  • Health & Wellbeing Coordinator
  • ISC Leadership Initiative (ILI) Coordinator
  • Marketing Coordinator
  • Programs Coordinator
  • State & Federal Government Liaison
  • Urbana City Liaison

Access the Coordinator & Liaison application! Application closes Tuesday, April 8th at Noon.

Legislative Officers
Legislative Officers provide leadership and oversight of the Congress, including guiding committees and Student Representatives. While the Student Representatives elect the Speaker and Deputy Speaker of the Student Congress at the start of the term, the Secretary and Parliamentarian are selected via application and interview. Learn more about Legislative Officers.

  • Secretary
  • Parliamentarian 

Access the Legislative Officer application! Application closes Thursday, April 16th at Noon.

At-Large Committee Members
At-Large Committee Members serve on one of the six Congress committees representing the student body's views. Each committee consists of two (2) At-Large Members.

Access the At-Large Committee Member application! Application closes Friday, April 4th at Noon.

Student Representatives
Student Representatives are selected by their respective colleges to represent students' interests and advocate for the betterment of the campus community.

Learn how to apply for a Student Representative vacancy!

Student Fee Advisory Committee
The Student Fee Advisory Committee advises the Vice Chancellor for Student Affairs on setting the amounts of student fees, excluding the General Fee

Learn more about SFAC here!
Apply for SFAC here!
