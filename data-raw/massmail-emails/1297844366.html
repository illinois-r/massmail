The Illini Cash program will end on June 30, 2026. 

Why is Illini Cash Discontinuing?
Students, faculty and staff members at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign now have easy access to various alternative payment options, including mobile payment apps, credit and debit cards, and other electronic payment methods widely accepted across campus. These convenient, contactless solutions have replaced the need for Illini Cash. 

Spend Down Your Account Balance
If you have an Illini Cash account, you are encouraged to spend down your remaining balance before the program ends on June 30, 2026. Illini Cash can be used to purchase food, printed materials, books, computer equipment and other select services across campus.
