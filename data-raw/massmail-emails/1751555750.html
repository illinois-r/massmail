Interested in Running for a Campus Elected Position?
January 29, 2025 9:30 PM

Fellow Students,

The Campus Student Election Commission (CSEC) is pleased to announce the start of the Spring 2025 Student Election Season! Voting will take place online beginning at 12:00 p.m. on Tuesday, February 25, through 11:59 a.m., Thursday, February 27, 2025.

Candidate applications, referendum submissions, and the Election timeline for the Spring 2025 election are now available on the CSEC’s website at https://studentelections.illinois.edu.

Candidate applications must be submitted no later than Monday, February 3, 2025, at 11:59 p.m. Students can apply to run for any of the following positions:

  • Illinois Student Council (ISC):
    • Student Body President & Vice President (as a ticket)
    • Student Representatives
  • The University Senate:
    • Student Senator
  • Student Organization Resource Fee (SORF) Board:
    • Member
  • Student Trustee

To complete the candidate application for any of these positions or learn more about the election process, visit https://studentelections.illinois.edu/candidate

The CSEC will be hosting regular office hours beginning this Friday, January 31 through February 28, 2025. Office hours can be attended in-person or virtually. Join us at CSEC Virtual Office Hours (password: elections) or by stopping by room 220 on the second floor of the University YMCA (1001 S. Wright Street). Office hours will be held on the following days:

  • Tuesday: 3:00 p.m. - 4:00 p.m.
  • Friday: 10:00 a.m. - 11:00 a.m.

If these times do not work for your schedule, email us anytime at studentelections@illinois.edu to set up a meeting.

Finally, if you are not interested in running for a position but want to be a part of the process, the CSEC is currently accepting membership applications at https://studentelections.illinois.edu/about. All are encouraged to apply! In addition, if you are interested in other un-elected positions, check out the University Senate Committees and apply today: https://www.senate.illinois.edu.

As always, if you have any questions, please contact the CSEC at studentelections@illinois.edu or stop by our regular office hours.

With Illini Pride,

The Campus Student Election Commission
