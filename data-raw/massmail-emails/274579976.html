ACTION REQUIRED: Voices for Change Training
July 7, 2023 3:02 PM

Welcome! We are thrilled that you are part of the University of Illinois family! 

At the University, sexual misconduct is not tolerated, and prevention efforts are ongoing and tremendously important. Hence, all new and continuing students are provided with annual training. We ask that you complete this training before you begin classes in August, to support your informed awareness and well-being in our campus community. 

Please be aware that any new students who do not complete the training will have a hold that prevents all registration activity placed on their student account for the upcoming semester until the coursework is complete. 

The course, Voices for Change, is the premier online program addressing the critical issues of sexual misconduct and creating an inclusive environment. With a focus on self-reflection and content application, Voices for Change is designed to help build academic communities of engaged bystanders who can identify harmful situations, and have the motivation and skills needed to intervene. 

Accessing the Course: 

Starting today until classes begin, students are able to self-register and complete the coursework. To do so, please follow the instructions below: 

Self-Registration Instructions:

  1. Open your web browser and go to https://go.uillinois.edu/sexual_misconduct_training
  2. Select your university location
  3. Enter your university net id and password
  4. Click the Sign In/Login button
  5. Authenticate using the 2FA (call, text, or passcode)
  6. Select the blue “Add modules” button under the “Your Courses” header.
  7. A pop-up window will appear and course listings will be available by campus
  8. Locate your campus and select either the Faculty and Staff Title IX Training (spring) or the Student training, Voices for Change - Comprehensive (Student), which is available year-round.
  9. Once you have located your course, place a check mark next to it and select the green “Add” button in the lower right corner. Your course will appear on your dashboard and will be available for completion.

Technical Difficulties? 

  • 24/7 support is available to users through the “Help” function inside the course.
  • Students having difficulty logging in to the course should call Technology Services at (217) 244-7000.
