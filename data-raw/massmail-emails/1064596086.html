2024 Campus Student Elections -> Voting Begins at Noon Today
February 27, 2024 8:21 AM

The Campus Student Election Commission (CSEC) is pleased to announce that voting will begin for the Spring 2024 Student Elections today at Noon! The ballot will be open from Tuesday, February 27, 2024 at 12:00 p.m. to Thursday, February 29, 2024 at Noon for all student elected positions.

Spring 2024 elections consist of the following:

  • The Student Trustee from the Urbana-Champaign Campus to the Board of Trustees of the University of Illinois System
  • Illinois Student Council (ISC) Student Body President and Vice President
  • Illinois Student Council (ISC) Student Representative – 44 seats representing all colleges
  • University Senate – 50 seats representing all colleges
  • 10 students to membership on the Student Organization Resource Fee Board (SORF)
  • All referenda submitted to a vote of the students of the Urbana-Champaign campus at the University of Illinois

Instructions to Access the Ballot:

  1. To access your Student Election Ballot, login to OneIllinois (one.illinois.edu).
  2. If it is the first time you have accessed OneIllinois, please follow the prompts to setup your profile.
  3. Once you have completed the profile setup, you may access the ballot by clicking on the Vote in Student Elections graphic below the OneIllinois banner.
  4. If you need assistance accessing your OneIllinois account, please email the OneIllinois Team at one@illinois.edu.

If you have any questions about the election process, please contact the CSEC at studentelections@illinois.edu.
