2023 Campus Student Elections - Voting Begins Tomorrow!

The Campus Student Election Commission (CSEC) is pleased to announce that voting will begin for the Spring 2023 Student Elections tomorrow! The ballot will open on Thursday, March 23, 2023 at 12:00 a.m. and close on Friday, March 24, 2023 at 11:59 p.m. for all student elected positions. 

The Spring 2023 election consists of the following:

  • The Student Trustee from the Urbana-Champaign Campus to the Board of Trustees of the University of Illinois System
  • Illinois Student Government (ISG) Student Body President and Vice President
  • Illinois Student Government (ISG) Senator – 44 seats representing all colleges
  • U-C Senate – 50 seats representing all colleges
  • 10 students to membership on the Student Organization Resource Fee Board (SORF)
  • All referenda submitted to a vote of the students of the Urbana-Champaign Campus at the University of Illinois

The ballot may be accessed at studentelections.illinois.edu. 

During peak times, the ballot may be slow to load.

If you have any questions, please contact the CSEC at studentelections@illinois.edu.
