Spring 2024 Campus Student Elections
January 24, 2024 5:01 AM

Fellow Students,

The Campus Student Election Commission (CSEC) is pleased to announce the start of the Spring 2024 Student Election Season! Voting will take place online beginning at 12:00 p.m. on Tuesday, February 27, through 11:59 a.m., Thursday, February 29, 2024.

Candidate applications, referendum submissions, the updated Student Election Code, and the Election timeline for the Spring 2024 election are now available on the CSEC’s website at https://studentelections.illinois.edu. 

Candidate applications must be submitted no later than Thursday, February 1, 2024, at 5:00 p.m. Students can apply to run for any of the following positions:

  • Illinois Student Council (ISC):
    • Student Body President & Vice President (as a ticket)
    • Student Representatives
  • The University Senate:
    • Student Senator
  • Student Organization Resource Fee (SORF) Board:
    • Member
  • Student Trustee

To complete the candidate application for any of these positions or learn more about the election process, visit https://studentelections.illinois.edu.  

If you are interested in running but not quite sure where to start, be sure to attend one of our info sessions this week. Sessions will be held each day from Tuesday, January 23, through Thursday, January 25, 2024, at 6:00 p.m virtually via Zoom. Register ahead of time by visiting: https://go.illinois.edu/CSECInfoSessions. After registering, you will receive a confirmation email containing information about joining the meeting. While attendance is not required, the CSEC encourages anyone interested in the election process to register as we will discuss important aspects of the process and campaign guidelines.

Can’t make the info sessions but have questions about the process? Already applied and need some help? No problem! The CSEC will be hosting regular office hours beginning this Monday, January 22 through March 22, 2024. Office hours can be attended in-person or virtually. Join us at https://go.illinois.edu/CSECOfficeHours2024 or by stopping by room 220 on the second floor of the University YMCA (1001 S. Wright Street).Cancellations will be noted on the CSEC Calendar. Office hours will be held on the following days:

  • Monday: 2:00-5:00 pm
  • Wednesday: 2:00-5:00 pm
  • Thursday: 9:00 am-12:00 pm

If these times do not work for your schedule, email us anytime at studentelections@illinois.edu to set up a meeting.

Finally, if you are not interested in running for a position but want to be a part of the process, the CSEC is currently accepting membership applications at https://studentelections.illinois.edu/about. All are encouraged to apply! In addition, if you are interested in other un-elected positions, check out the University Senate Committees and apply today: https://www.senate.illinois.edu. 

As always, If you have any questions, please contact the CSEC at studentelections@illinois.edu or stop by our regular office hours.

With Illini Pride,

The Campus Student Election Commission
