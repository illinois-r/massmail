Student Insurance Updates for Fall 2025
April 24, 2025 5:02 AM

YOUR ACTION IS REQUIRED. SEE BELOW.

Dear Student,

The Enrollment/Waiver Periods, Coverage Dates, Fees, and Plan Summaries for Fall 2025 are now available on the Student Insurance Website. While fees are increasing 3.5%, the plan benefits remain unchanged. There are no increases to deductibles, out-of-pocket maximums (OOPM), etc. Please review benefit plan details for complete information.

Need Help?

The Student Health Insurance staff is here to ensure you have all the information needed to make the right decision about your healthcare coverage needs. Please do not hesitate to connect with us if you have additional questions.

Location: 1109 S. Lincoln Ave. Urbana, IL 61801 (Inside McKinley Health Center in the main lobby.) 

Hours: Monday-Friday from 8:00am-4:30pm

Contact Us:
