Invitation to Apply for Undergrad Student Advisory Board
September 3, 2025 3:01 PM

Dear Students,  

Are you looking for opportunities to get involved and work together with peers and campus leadership to strengthen our community? If so, we have the perfect opportunity for you! 

The application for the 2025-2026 Student Success Undergraduate Advisory Board is now live. All applications are due Friday, September 12, 2025, at 11:59 PM CDT. To be considered, students must be enrolled as a full-time, degree-seeking undergraduate and be on campus for both the fall and spring semesters.

The board is a group of diverse undergraduate students who volunteer to serve as advisors to the Office of the Provost & Executive Vice Chancellor and Student Success @ Illinois. They aim to accurately portray the current realities of the undergraduate experience by articulating the highlights and challenges of being a student at the university. As representatives of the undergraduate population at Illinois, their objective is to convey what could make a successful undergraduate student experience.  

We hope you will consider applying because your voice is a critical part of the work that we do to ensure your success while at Illinois and beyond graduation!

Sincerely,  

Kathy Martensen
Associate Provost for Educational Programs and Success Initiatives
