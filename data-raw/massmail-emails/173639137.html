September 28, Student Mental Health Symposium
September 23, 2022 7:57 AM

Dear colleagues, 

There is still time to register for this year's virtual, free Student Mental Health Symposium. The symposium is part of ongoing efforts to advance our response to the mental health needs of our university communities, especially the students. 

We look forward to your participation in this important continued conversation. Please complete the registration form to attend the symposium online.

When:                 Wednesday, September 28, 2022
Time:                  10:00 am to noon CST
Audience:           Faculty, staff, and student leaders across the University of Illinois System
Speaker:             Dr. Stacey Pearson-Wharton

For nearly 20 years, Dr. Stacey Pearson-Wharton or Dr. Stacey (as her students affectionately call her) has been dedicated to helping students maximize their learning, health and wellness, and personal growth. Now as a speaker, she visits universities across the country to inspire and educate students on taking steps for positive change in their lives and for their communities.
