Spring 2026 Campus Student Elections
January 28, 2026 3:02 PM

Fellow Students,

The Student Election Board (SEB) is pleased to announce the start of the Spring 2026 Student Election Season! Voting will take place online beginning at 12:00 p.m. on Tuesday, February 17, 2026, through 11:59 AM, Thursday, February 19, 2026.

Candidate applications, referendum submissions, and the Election timeline for the Spring 2026 election are now available on the SEB’s website at https://studentelections.illinois.edu. 

Candidate applications must be submitted no later than Tuesday, February 3, 2026, at 12:00 p.m. 

Students can apply to run for any of the following positions:

  • Illinois Student Council (ISC):
    • Student Body President & Vice President (as a ticket)
    • Student Representatives
  • The University Senate:
    • Student Senator
  • Student Organization Resource Fee (SORF) Board:
    • Member
  • Student Trustee

To complete the candidate application for any of these positions or learn more about the election process, visit https://studentelections.illinois.edu/candidate. 

The SEB will be hosting candidate informational meetings:

  • January 27-28 from 7-8pm in the Illini Union, Pine Lounge
  • January 29 from 7-8pm in the Illini Union, Room 103
  • February 10-11 in the Lincoln Hall, Theater from 7-9pm  

If these times do not work for your schedule, email us anytime at studentelections@illinois.edu to set up a meeting.

In addition, if you are interested in other un-elected positions, check out the University Senate Committees and apply today at https://www.senate.illinois.edu. 

As always, If you have any questions, please contact the SEB at studentelections@illinois.edu or stop by our regular office hours.

With Illini Pride,

The Student Election Committee
