The Bloomington Gold Corvette Show will be at the State Farm Center next Tuesday, June 25 through Sunday, June 30 and will have exclusive access to all four parking quadrants as well as a few additional lots near the building. The show hours for this event are June 28-30 from 7 a.m. until 5 p.m. Thousands of spectators are expected each day.

During this event, there will be a number of street closures around State Farm Center. For a map of the street closures including days and times these streets will be closed, visit www.uofiassemblyhall.com.

Please keep this information in mind when planning your travel near this part of campus during this time period.
